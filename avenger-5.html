Description

During World War II, Steve Rogers volunteered to receive the experimental Super-Soldier Serum.

Enhanced to the pinnacle of human physical potential and armed with an unbreakable shield, he became Captain America.

After a failed mission left him encased in ice for decades, he was found and revived by the Avengers,

later joining their ranks and eventually becoming the team's leader.
